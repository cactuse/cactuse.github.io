EXPERIMENT TRANSLATION

Creative summer residency

2015, Lithuania

When Lithuanian Centre for Human Rights asked us to help fight racial and homophobic hate speech online and in mass media, we knew we needed to deliver a communications campaign that can be impactful on a personal level.

Social Experiment TRANSLATION was shot with real people, unsuspecting they were being filmed and capturing their reactions faced with racism in a real-life situation.

We knew we hit the empathy strings hard when the video went viral the next day, reaching 4,300,000+ viewers on YouTube on its first week with zero media budget (yes, this was still possible back in 2015!)

The project won Silver at Golden Drum advertising festival in Slovenia, and was picked up by national and international mass media, including THE INDEPENDENT, DER SPIEGEL, AL JAZEERA, ZDF, TV GLOBO INTERNATIONAL, SBS, REDDIT, WHAT’S TRENDING, UPWORTHY, 9GAG, etc.
